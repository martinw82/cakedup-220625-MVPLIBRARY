# Welcome to Cake-Up! - User Guide

Hello and welcome to Cake-Up, your delightful corner for discovering amazing cake recipes and finding the best baking products! Whether you're a seasoned baker or just starting out, Cake-Up is here to inspire your next baking adventure.

## What is Cake-Up?

Cake-Up is a user-friendly website where you can:

*   **Discover Recipes:** Browse through a collection of delicious cake recipes.
*   **Get Inspired:** Find new ideas for cakes, from classic flavors to creative concoctions.
*   **Shop for Baking Goods:** Explore a curated selection of baking products to help you create your masterpieces.

## Navigating the Site

Cake-Up is designed to be easy and intuitive to use. Here’s a quick look at the main sections:

*   **Home:** This is where your journey begins! You'll find featured recipes and highlights to get you started.
*   **Recipes:** Dive into our recipe collection. You can browse all recipes or look for something specific.
*   **Recipe Detail:** When you click on a recipe, you'll see its detailed page with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and baking tips.
*   **Shop:** Visit our shop to find quality baking ingredients and tools.

## How to Use Cake-Up

### 1. Finding a Recipe

*   **Visit the "Recipes" Page:** Click on "Recipes" in the main navigation menu (usually at the top of the page).
*   **Browse:** Scroll through the list of available recipes. Each recipe will typically show a picture and its name.
*   **View Details:** Click on a recipe card that interests you. This will take you to the "Recipe Detail" page.

### 2. Understanding a Recipe Page

Once you're on a recipe's detail page, you'll find:

*   **Recipe Name & Image:** A clear title and a picture of the finished cake.
*   **Description:** A brief overview of the cake.
*   **Ingredients List:** All the necessary ingredients and their quantities.
*   **Instructions:** Step-by-step guidance on how to prepare and bake the cake.
*   **Baking Time & Difficulty:** Information to help you plan.

Read through the ingredients and instructions carefully before you start baking.

### 3. Exploring the Shop

*   **Visit the "Shop" Page:** Click on "Shop" in the main navigation menu.
*   **Browse Products:** Look through the available baking products. Each product will have a name, image, and price.
*   **(Future Feature) Product Details:** Clicking on a product might show more details, reviews, or options (if available).
*   **(Future Feature) Adding to Cart:** In a full e-commerce setup, you'd be able to add products to a shopping cart and proceed to checkout. Currently, the shop showcases products.

## Tips for Users

*   **Check Back Often:** We plan to add new recipes and products, so visit us regularly!
*   **Read Reviews (if available):** Once user reviews are implemented, they can provide helpful insights into recipes and products.

## Troubleshooting & Support

*   **Page Not Loading Correctly?** Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, check your internet connection.
*   **Questions or Feedback?** While direct support channels might not be established for this MVP, your feedback is valuable. If there's a contact method provided on the site (e.g., in the Footer), please use it.

We hope you enjoy using Cake-Up! Happy baking!
